Brief Description

Noida is a planned city located in Gautam Buddha Nagar district of the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh
Noida, short for New Okhla Industrial Development Authority.

Noida History

Noida was ranked as the Best City in Uttar Pradesh and the Best City in Housing in all of India in the "Best City Awards" conducted by ABP News in 2015.
Noida is considered to be India's greenest city with nearly 50% green cover, the highest of any city in India
